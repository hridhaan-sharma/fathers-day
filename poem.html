By Hridhaan Sharma , Your loving son

My Dear Papa,

You gave me all the things I needed

Made delicious food whenever I pleaded


You dedicated every second for me, even if you were restless

That is why a father's love is always priceless


The care you took of me and the fun I had with you

Was the reason that made me see you as the best father from my point of view


Well words can't fullfil the love of yours

But I hope my feelings would touch you like the pleasant sound of the water beside the shores


And I hope you would stay humble and kind for years and mores
